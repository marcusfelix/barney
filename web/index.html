// Prehistoric AI Assistant

Barney

Once upon a time, in the age of terminals and command lines, a new species emerged from the primordial code. Barney is an intelligent CLI companion that evolved alongside OpenCode, learning to assist developers in the wild.

Extracted from ancient amber and rebuilt with Bun, this paleontologist of productivity digs through your code to unearth bugs, discovers patterns in the fossil record of your git history, and brings your development workflow back from extinction.

// Capabilities

  • Headless OpenCode integration for seamless AI assistance
  • Real-time TUI with live logs and interactive prompts
  • Monitors your workspace like a dinosaur watching for prey
  • Works autonomously on tasks defined in .context/tasks/*.md
  • Role-based persona system (developer, and more to fossilize)

// Operation Modes

ghost
Passive monitoring. Stands still like a statue in amber.
coworker
Watches changes, offers insights. Like a helpful pack member.
auto
Autonomous task execution. Hunts down todos relentlessly.
